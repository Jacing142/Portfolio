About

Built around how people actually work

I came to AI engineering from education and psychology, so I start with people: how a team actually works, where the hours go, and what a system needs to do to be trusted.

Production systems fail in boring ways. Someone uploads the wrong file, an API times out, an edge case shows up on day one. I build for that path as much as the happy one: clear escalation to humans, outputs checked before they ship, nothing your team can't override.

I work remotely and in person with clients across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and APAC. One standard applies to everything: measurably less manual work, and nothing broken along the way. I also maintain GenAI Conversation Explorer ↗, an open-source tool used by 100+ people weekly.
